## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

To access the full features of SoleBlessing including shopping cart, wishlist, and order tracking, you need to create an account. Click the **Account** icon in the top navigation bar and select **Login**. You will be redirected to the secure Manus authentication portal where you can sign up using your email address or social media accounts.

The authentication process is handled through Manus OAuth, ensuring your credentials are protected by industry-standard security measures. Once authenticated, you will be automatically redirected back to SoleBlessing with your session active.

**Product Categories**: Navigate to the **Products** page using the main navigation menu to browse the complete catalog. Use the filter panel on the left side to narrow results by brand, category, price range, and size availability.

**Recommended Products**: Based on your viewing history, the homepage displays personalized recommendations featuring products similar to those you've previously browsed.

## Shopping Cart

### Adding Items to Cart

From any product detail page, select your desired size using the size buttons and adjust the quantity using the plus and minus controls. The quantity defaults to 1 but can be increased up to the available stock limit. Click the **Add to Cart** button to add the item to your shopping cart.

A confirmation toast notification appears in the bottom-right corner indicating the item was successfully added. The cart icon in the top navigation updates to show the total number of items in your cart.

### Managing Your Cart

Click the shopping cart icon in the top navigation to view your cart contents. The cart page displays all items you've added with the following information for each:

**Product Image**: A thumbnail of the sneaker for quick visual identification.

**Product Details**: Name, brand, selected size, and unit price.

**Quantity Controls**: Plus and minus buttons allow you to adjust quantities directly in the cart. Changes are saved automatically.

**Remove Option**: Click the trash icon to remove an item from your cart entirely.

**Price Calculation**: The subtotal for each item (quantity Ã— unit price) appears on the right side. The cart total at the bottom sums all item subtotals.

### Proceeding to Checkout

When you're ready to complete your purchase, click the **Proceed to Checkout** button at the bottom of the cart page. This button is only enabled when your cart contains at least one item.

## Wishlist

### Saving Products for Later

If you find a product you're interested in but not ready to purchase immediately, add it to your wishlist by clicking the heart icon on the product detail page. The heart fills with color to indicate the item is saved.

### Accessing Your Wishlist

View your saved products by clicking the heart icon in the top navigation. The wishlist page displays all products you've saved with options to:

**Add to Cart**: Click the shopping cart button on any wishlist item to move it directly to your cart with a default size and quantity of 1.

**Remove from Wishlist**: Click the heart icon again to remove the product from your wishlist.

**View Details**: Click the product card to return to the full product detail page.

Your wishlist persists across sessions, so you can save products and return to them later even after logging out.

## Checkout Process

### Entering Shipping Information

The checkout page collects the information needed to process and deliver your order. Fill in the following required fields:

**Full Name**: Your complete name as it should appear on the shipping label.

**Email Address**: A valid email where you'll receive order confirmation and updates.

**Phone Number**: Contact number for delivery coordination.

**Complete Shipping Address**: Include street address, barangay, city, province, and postal code. Be as detailed as possible to ensure successful delivery.

### Selecting Payment Method

SoleBlessing currently accepts two payment methods:

**Bank Transfer**: Direct bank deposit to our designated account. After selecting this option, bank account details will be displayed including account name, account number, and bank name.

**GCash**: Mobile wallet payment through GCash. The GCash number and account name will be displayed for payment.

### Uploading Payment Proof

After completing your payment through your chosen method, you must upload proof of payment to confirm your order. Click the **Choose File** button and select a clear photo or screenshot of your payment receipt. Accepted file formats include JPG, PNG, and PDF with a maximum size of 5MB.

The payment proof should clearly show:

- Transaction date and time
- Amount paid
- Reference number
- Recipient account details

Once uploaded, the filename appears next to the upload button confirming your file is ready for submission.

### Placing Your Order

Review all information carefully before submitting. The order summary on the right side displays:

- All items with sizes and quantities
- Individual item prices
- Order subtotal
- Total amount due

Click the **Place Order** button to submit your order. You'll see a confirmation message and your cart will be automatically cleared. Your order is now pending admin verification of the payment proof.

## Order Tracking

### Viewing Order History

Access your order history by clicking your account icon in the top navigation and selecting **My Orders**. This page displays all orders you've placed with the following information:

**Order Number**: Unique identifier for tracking purposes (format: #12345).

**Order Date**: When the order was placed.

**Total Amount**: Final order value in Philippine pesos.

**Payment Method**: Bank transfer or GCash.

**Order Status**: Current state of your order with color-coded badges:
- **Pending** (yellow): Awaiting payment verification by admin
- **Processing** (blue): Payment confirmed, order being prepared
- **Shipped** (purple): Order dispatched for delivery
- **Delivered** (green): Order successfully delivered

### Understanding Order Status

**Pending Status**: After placing your order, it enters pending status while our team verifies your payment proof. This typically takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive an email notification once payment is confirmed.

**Processing Status**: Once payment is verified, your order moves to processing status. Our warehouse team prepares your items for shipment, which usually takes 2-3 business days.

**Shipped Status**: When your order is dispatched, you'll receive a notification with tracking information. Delivery time depends on your location but typically ranges from 3-7 business days within Metro Manila and 5-10 business days for provincial deliveries.

**Delivered Status**: After successful delivery, your order status updates to delivered. If you encounter any issues with your order, contact our customer service team through the inquiry form.

For questions about product authenticity, condition, or specifications, use the same inquiry form. Our customer service team has extensive knowledge of our inventory and can provide detailed information to help you make informed purchasing decisions.
